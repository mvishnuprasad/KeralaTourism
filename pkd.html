Experience Palakkad


Palakkad is a city in the state of Kerala, on India's Malabar Coast. Itâ€™s known as a trade hub and for its beaches, Flanked by the Western Ghats on the east and the Vembanad Lake and paddy fields of Kuttanad on the west, Palakkad is a place that is known for extraordinary qualities. Nearby, Ashtamudi Lake is a gateway to the Kerala backwaters, a network of waterways rich with vegetation. The striped 1902 Tangasseri Lighthouse has ocean views.

The district has three major rivers, several freshwater lakes, and over 300 ponds. Its eastern region is forested, northern regions are mostly under rubber cultivation and the remaining areas grow mixed dry-land crops of coconut, plantain, and tapioca, among others. Built-up areas and rice fields complete the land use pattern
